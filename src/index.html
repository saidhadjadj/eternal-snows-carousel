Grand Froid et Neiges Éternelles

🌍 Project Overview

This responsive web carousel showcases stunning high-resolution images of snow-capped peaks, glacial valleys, and frozen tundras - the ultimate embodiment of "Grand Froid et Neiges Éternelles". Each image pivots smoothly on hover (3-second GPU-accelerated 3D rotation) to reveal detailed presentations on the verso.

  • 20+ curated images from premium free sources
  • 4K snow-covered mountains at golden hour, blue hour
  • Glacial crevasses, ice caves, polar expeditions themes
  • Silky smooth 3D flip with perspective: 1000px
